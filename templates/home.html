What is Money Stew ?


MoneyStew will help you keep your money and bills a bit more organized and keep a better idea on just how much money you have.


MoneyStew will help you keep track of repeating transactions, what days they are due, and let you know if you will have enough money to pay all your bills before you get paid again (although I know it sucks when then answer is no). It will help you get a grip on how much money you already spend on bills from month to month and how much you can spend on beer and bacon. (you should always budget for beer and bacon)

Why use MoneyStew?
If you have trouble keeping track of your finances and refuse to balance your checkbook weekly, then MoneyStew is for you. I built MoneyStew to help me keep track of what bills I have to pay in the next few weeks and I don't want to waste time updating it each week. I just wanted to login, and see if I have enough money to make it without running my account dry.

How do I use MoneyStew?
Just login and add all your repeating payments and then the next time you want to buy a new tv, computer or yearly supply of bacon, you pull up MoneyStew and check out if you have enough money to pay your bills and purchase that scrumptious chunk of meat before you get paid again. You dont have to keep updating, well, when your payments change you do.

I built money stew to help me keep track of my own money and I figured it might be something other people may find useful. I found myself continually making spread sheets of my repeating transactions to help me budget my money and determine how much I could actually spend. So I finally decided to make a small application that would let me jump in and take a look at what I had going on.

What MoneyStew Is Not

MoneyStew is a simple tool to help keep a bit more organized, it is not really intentended to do much more than keep a list of your bills and income all in one place.

  • A detailed finacial calculator-MoneyStew isn't going to help tell you how to spend your money, mostly it will just tell you when to spend your money
  • A full money managment application - MoneyStew isn't like Mint.com or Quicken, it is just a simple tool to store your repeating income and payments.
  • A Giant Dragon - MoneyStew is not a large dragon that will destroy cities (yet)
Anyway, use your google login and check it out, it may help you.
