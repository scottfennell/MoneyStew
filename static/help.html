Money Stew Help



What is Money Stew

Money Stew will help you keep track of your income and expenses. The intention is to allow you to get a quick snapshot of your finances at any given time.



Getting Started

  • Click Add Transaction to add a new repeating payment
  • Fill out the form and then click save to add it to your list
  • Add all your repeating transactions, including income, make sure you select the "income?" checkbox if you want the amount to add to your balance
  • You can click on the edit icon to edit a payment, or the delete icon to delete
  • Enter your current account balance and hit Recalculate to show your projected account balance, this information is not saved, it is just used for calculation purposes
  • Check the Paid checkbox to mark an individual payment as already paid, this is only to help calculate your projected balance and is not saved
